Project name:

PumpItUp

Published on:

18th September 2012

In production:

No

Description:

Today, most of the loudspeakers are big in size due to their large diaphragm. Hence, they limit their portability. The idea is to replace the conventional diaphragm with a more elastic diaphragm so that it can be compressed or stretched, without altering the sound and vibration quality, thus making it portable.

Its circular radius is same as of a compact disc i.e. 60mm, and height is 20mm (when closed).

It has in-built :
  1. Bluetooth device
  2. Music player
  3. MMC card slot
  4. Battery
  5. Battery status indicator
  6. USB charging
