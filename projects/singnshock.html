Project name:

singNshock

Published on:

18th September 2012

In production:

No

Description:

singNshock is a capacitive touch screen alarm clock that features an optional electric shock feature, which gives micro-volts of electric current, when the button (aluminum coated) on the top of the clock is pushed to turn off the alarm. The shock triggers neurological functions of the human body and the user gets conscious and thus wakes up. It also features an integrated music player with mmc slot. It allows you to store unlimited music so that you can wake up to the melodies of your favorite song.

The intention behind designing a functional desk alarm clock with pleasing aesthetics was to keep mobile phones away from the user at night. As mobile phones always emit radiation, whether you make a call or not.
