Project name:

270 Degree

Published on:

18th September 2012

In production:

No

Description:

270 Degree is named for the degree by which its 3 circular pipe mechanisms swing. It's a functional stool design which is actually an all-in-one chair, desk and table. It’s the perfect multipurpose piece for both compact spaces and modern offices, effortlessly transitioning from seat to workstation with a simple pull of one of the panels.
