Project name:

Bibo

Published on:

14th July 2013

In production:

No

Description:

Bibo is an analog wrist watch which aims to reduce the use of wall clocks in houses where the house owners are involved in a work culture of 9-5, as wall clocks are less used in these houses. The clock can be docked in a wrist strap to use it as a wrist watch or in a desk-docking device which projects the current time on the clock using an in-built mini-projector, replacing your traditional wall clock. The desk-docking device uses a rechargeable-battery, built inside the docking device, for projection.

Also, to bring back the essence of long forgotten Indian painting style, the wrist strap and docking device are graphically combined with Madhubani art's geometric patterns and the name is taken from "BIBOSWAN", male name of Indian origins meaning Sun God.
